# Code of Conduct

## Scope

This repository is part of a ecosystem of open source projects developed & published by [frozen-lab](https://github.com/frozen-lab/)
and is intended to support those systems. While it is publicly available and open to contributions, its design and
direction are guided by its role within that ecosystem.

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within this repository, including issues, discussions, pull requests,
and any other form of communication.

## Nature of Project

- This project is **open source and accepts all contributions**.
- It is designed to serve the **ecosystem of related crates**.
- Usage outside this intended scope is **discouraged but not prohibited**.

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to,

- Maintain **technical clarity and precision** in discussions.
- Be **respectful and concise** in communication.
- Focus on **relevant, constructive, and actionable input**.
- Align contributions with the **project’s goals and design philosophy**.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated,

- Harassment, personal attacks, or disrespectful language.
- Low-quality, vague, or non-actionable issues or pull requests.
- Disruptive behavior or attempts to derail technical discussions.


## Contributions

- Contributions are **welcome and encouraged**.
- The maintainer(s) retains final decision making authority to ensure **consistency and long term maintainability**
  of the project.

## Guidelines for Issues

When opening an issue,

- Ensure it is **directly relevant** to the project.
- Provide **clear, minimal, and reproducible details**.
- Avoid speculative or overly broad discussions.

## Enforcement

The maintainer(s) reserves the right to,

- Remove or ignore content that does not comply with this Code of Conduct.
- Take appropriate action in case of repeated or severe violations.
